Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a CPR course? expand_more

To enroll in a CPR course:

  1. Browse available courses on the home page or use the search feature
  2. Select your preferred date, time, and location
  3. Click "Enroll Now" and create an account if you haven't already
  4. Complete the payment process
  5. You'll receive a confirmation email with course details
What is your cancellation policy? expand_more

Our cancellation policy:

  • Full refund if cancelled 48+ hours before class
  • 50% refund if cancelled 24-48 hours before class
  • No refund if cancelled less than 24 hours before class
  • Free rescheduling available up to 24 hours before class
How do I access my digital certificate? expand_more

After successfully completing your course:

  1. Log into your account dashboard
  2. Go to "My Certifications" section
  3. Click "Download" next to your certificate
  4. You can also add it to your digital wallet for easy access

Certificates are typically available within 24 hours of course completion.

What should I bring to my CPR class? expand_more

Please bring the following to your CPR class:

  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Confirmation email (printed or on your phone)
  • Comfortable clothing suitable for practicing on the floor
  • Any required pre-course materials or textbooks
  • Note-taking materials (optional)
How long is my CPR certification valid? expand_more

Certification validity periods:

  • BLS Provider: 2 years
  • ACLS Provider: 2 years
  • PALS Provider: 2 years
  • Heartsaver First Aid: 2 years
  • Heartsaver CPR AED: 2 years

We'll send you renewal reminders starting 60 days before expiration.
